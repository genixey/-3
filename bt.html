Brewing Trouble

Brewing Trouble is a narrative-driven alchemy simulator where you step into the worn shoes of a reclusive alchemist trying to revive their business one strange customer at a time.

Inspired by Potion Craft, Papers, Please, and Presentable Liberty, this game blends potion making mechanics with time-based decisions and light resource management.

Set in a quiet, isolated world, each day brings new orders, new challenges, and a chance to refine your skills.
